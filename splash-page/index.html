How to Use SearchParty
  1. Sign up for a free account
    We are proud to utilize state of the art encryption and security measures to keep your information secure.
  2. Select the hunt you would like to join
    Have a blast while you complete tasks at different locations, and don't forget to upload pictures directly to your hunt!
  3. Share your hunt with your friends
    Use the generated url to share a live stream of the hunt, directly to twitter or to your favorite social media platform.
